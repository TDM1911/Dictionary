This is a Eng-Vie/Vie-Eng dictionary program.

It contains over 100,000 entries in English, and 140,000 entries in Vietnamese.
The dictionary also include more than 7000 quality entries of basic English pronunciation.

We're trying to create a friendly interface to help users to add, edit or remove unwanted words easily.

Check 'How to use' in the 'Help' menu for more detail on how to use the dictionary.
