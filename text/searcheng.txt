How to search:
 - Type in the search box the word you need to find.
 - You can see a list of sugggestion word under the box.
 - To choose a sugggestion, press the arrow key. Press Enter to pick the word.
 - There're many sugggestion mode. Check the Menubar help for more detail.
 - Once you done, press Enter to search. You will see the word on the right box an the meaning in the box below called the meaning box.
 Each line will be color coded differently to help you find what you need easily.
NOTE: Make sure you are on the right dictionary mode.
You can see by checking in the Option Menu/Dictionary mode or check at the top right corner of the screeen. 
You can also use Ctrl+Enter for a quick dictionary switch.
